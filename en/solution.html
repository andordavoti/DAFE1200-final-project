«Sustainable information for all»

cFood provides consumers with an overview and guidance for more sustainable consumption by replacing certain foods with alternatives that have a lower footprint. The products are evaluated in four categories using different parameters:

Environmental impact

Total CO 2 emissions from production, transport, water consumption and land use of the product.[5]

Ecology

Based on factors of production such as the use of synthetic pesticides and fertilizers. We make use of underlying facts from existing ones marking, for example Debio's Ø mark. [6]

Ethics

Whether workers and animals are treated in a dignified manner. Based on labeles, such as Fairtrade and the Animal Welfare label. [6]

Recycling

A comparison of different packaging on similar products, and the products' possibilities for recycling. Based on marking from Grønt Punkt Norge. [6]

You save money, together we protect the environment

We give you another good reason to get started with the solution the challenges: You can save money. The app gives you challenges based on your consumption, and if you meet the challenges you are rewarded with discount coupons on foods, which are sustainable.
